We understand there can be good reasons why a practice might prescribe more or less of something than other practices.

OpenPrescribing is a free, publicly-funded service created by researchers in the University of Oxford to make national prescribing data more useful. You can see the performance of your practice on various prescribing measures, look at any practice or CCG in the country, and do bespoke data searches on any treatment of interest.
